General Questions

The Dairy Management System is a platform connecting dairy businesses with customers. Users can browse products, place orders, participate in community discussions, and contact franchise owners. Dairy businesses can manage their operations, inventory, and customer relationships.

Yes, you need a free account to browse products, participate in discussions, and contact franchise owners. Creating an account is simple - just click "Sign Up" and follow the registration process.

For Users

  • ✓ Browse dairy products and view details
  • ✓ Participate in community discussions
  • ✓ Contact franchise owners
  • ✓ Submit support tickets
  • ✓ View product availability and pricing
  • ✓ Track order status

Click on your profile icon in the top right corner, select "Profile Settings," and you can update your personal information, notification preferences, and communication settings.

Products & Orders

Visit the Products page to browse all available dairy products. You can filter by category, location, or price range. Each product listing shows detailed information including price, availability, and quality metrics.

Go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard to view all your orders and their current status. You can see delivery estimates, order details, and contact support if needed.

Community & Discussions

Visit the Community section to view ongoing discussions. You can read topics, reply to existing threads, and search for specific topics. Remember to follow our community guidelines when participating.

Yes, you can start new discussion topics in the community forum. Click "New Topic," choose a relevant category, and share your question or discussion point with the community.

Franchise Inquiries

Visit the franchise's profile page and click "Contact Owner" to send them a message. You can inquire about products, services, or business opportunities through this channel.

To submit a franchise inquiry, go to the Franchise section and click "Submit Inquiry." Fill out the form with your details and business interests, and our franchise development team will contact you.

Support

You can get help by:
  • Creating a support ticket
  • Using live chat during business hours
  • Checking our FAQ section
  • Posting in the community forum
  • Calling our support hotline for urgent issues

Click on "Support" in the navigation menu, then select "Create Ticket." Describe your issue in detail, attach any relevant screenshots, and submit. Our support team will respond within 24 hours.
